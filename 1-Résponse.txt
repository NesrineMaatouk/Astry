This company aims to make life easier for artists and art lovers by developing a platform/application that will revolutionize the art field by introducing new technology.

This mobile platform/app allows art lovers to answer relevant questions, evaluate results and better anticipate future probabilities and trends. Example: discover the history of artists, details of their artworks, their paintings and many other information.....

In addition it allows to extract data from different datasets (API..) and other data generated by machine learning/data mining. It will contain a part of artificial intelligence and machine learning for the estimation of data collections. 

This platform/application will be in English, it is dedicated to 90% for ASI countries and 10% for US countries. 
Python and Javascript programming technologies.
